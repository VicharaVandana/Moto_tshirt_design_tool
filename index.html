How To Guide

  1. First choose the Tshirt type from drop down like Short sleeve tshirts or Hoodies etc from T-Shirt Type section
  2. Now Choose the colour of the cloth from colour pallete in T-Shirt Colour section
  3. Switch to Design tab. Here you can add text and your custom images to the t-shirt
  4. For adding the Text, you can type in text box and click on arrow icon to put it on tshirt.
  5. Once the text is on the t-shirt you can select them and change its properties with pallete appearing when you select the text.
  6. For adding the image, click on Browse Image button and select the image from your PC. Only Jpg and PNG images are supported right now.
  7. Once the image is uploaded, Click on the image and itwillappear on Tshirt. You can resize and move and rotate it there as you like.
  8. To swap the front and back of tshirt, there is a rotate View icon.
  9. If you want to delete an item on Tshirt, then click on the item, a pallete will appear at right top. There click delete button.
  10. Once your design is ready, Click on Export Design button, The image will be added below. Multiple designs can be added too.
  11. Once the Design is added below, you can rightclick on the image and save it as PNG image locally and share it with your t-shirt designer.
  12. If you want to clear the exported designs, then click on Clear Designs button and all the designs exported will be cleared and you can export fresh designs
